APEX-BUILD is a multi-agent AI cloud IDE built to win on cost discipline, product depth, and customer control.

APEX-BUILD is positioned against incumbent AI coding platforms by addressing the complaints that push customers to look elsewhere: black-box orchestration, weak recovery, shallow editing, hidden spend, and platform lock-in. This raise is structured as $10,000 for 5% to complete launch preparation, formalize the business, keep the AI rails funded, and push customer acquisition.

Credit burn reduction is the migration lever.

Credit burn means how quickly paid credits or AI budget are consumed during a build. APEX-BUILD has already reduced that burn by about 30% so far, and the goal is to push toward 50% lower burn than Replit. Lower burn means more usable product output for the same spend, which is one of the clearest reasons to migrate.

BYOK means Bring Your Own Keys: customers can plug in their own Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, Grok, or Ollama-backed setup and use APEX-BUILD as the orchestration layer instead of paying only through platform-managed credits.
