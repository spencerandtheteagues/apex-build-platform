- Dashboard with pipeline value, weighted forecast, win rate, open deals, and follow-up metrics.

- Deals pipeline Kanban with draggable cards across Prospecting, Qualified, Proposal, Negotiation, Won, and Lost.
- Deal detail page with contact info, activity timeline, notes, tasks, next step, and value calculator.
- New deal form with validation and live forecast calculation.
- Settings page with stages, owners, default probability, and reset demo data.

Use realistic demo data, instant interactions, responsive layout, success toasts, smooth states, and no console errors.
