- Sensitivity analysis with sliders for adoption rate, cost variance, and growth rate.
- Saved scenarios page with compare, duplicate, delete, and reset demo data.

Use correct calculations, clear formulas, realistic defaults, responsive UI, accessible inputs, error states, and no console errors.
